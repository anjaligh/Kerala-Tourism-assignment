-Kerala Food-

Kerala's love for sea food is as legendary as the mouthwatering variety of sea food dishes available in the state. ... Many of the fish recipes of Kerala like Karimeen Pollichatu, Chemmeen roast, stuffed Mussels and Fish Moilee have cult status among ardent fish lovers from within and without the state.

-Culture & Tradition-

The culture of Kerala is a combination of both Indian and Dravidian culture. The essence of Kerala lies in its performing arts like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam and Martial Art of Kalaripayattu.

-Festivals-

Kerala has got various Festivals of different religions – from backwaters to the most colorful festivals in India. Festivals in Kerala are celebrated with great zeal and enthusiasm, as they speak volumes about the state's history, culture and beliefs.
